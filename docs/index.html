What We Do

At Green Tint Solutions, we specialize in providing high-quality chemical solutions for two distinct industries: Textile and Automotive. Our expertise lies in delivering innovative and effective products that cater to the unique needs of each sector.

Textile Chemicals

We offer a comprehensive range of textile chemicals that enhance the quality, durability, and aesthetic appeal of fabrics. Our products include:

  • Silicon Softners: Enhancing fabric performance with innovative textile chemicals
  • Finishing Auxiliaries: Advanced finishing auxiliaries for enhanced fabric softness, smoothness, and wrinkle resistance.
  • Water Repellent: Advanced water repellent chemical for durable and long-lasting water resistance.
  • Oil and Water Repellent: Revolutionary oil and water repellent chemical for ultimate stain protection and durability.
  • Yarn Lubricants: High-performance yarn lubricants for reduced friction, improved yarn runnability, and enhanced textile quality.
  • Flame Retardant: Innovative flame retardant solutions for enhanced fire safety, durability, and compliance with global standards.

Automotive Chemicals

Our automobile chemicals are designed to meet the demanding requirements of the automotive industry. Our product range includes:

  • Water Based Mold Release Agent: Eco-friendly water-based mold release agent for efficient mold separation, reduced residue, and improved product quality.
  • Silicon Based Mold Release Agent: High-performance silicone-based mold release agent for effortless mold separation, reduced sticking, and extended mold life.
  • Foam Generating Agent: Powerful foam generating agent for creating rich, stable, and long-lasting foam in various textile and industrial applications.
